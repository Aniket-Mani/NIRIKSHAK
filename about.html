About NIRIKSHAK

**NIRIKSHAK** is a cutting-edge **smart system** designed to **revolutionize the evaluation of student answer scripts** in schools and colleges. Leveraging advanced **Artificial Intelligence**, it automates the process of checking answers, assigning marks, and delivering results with unprecedented speed and accuracy.

Our innovative approach significantly **reduces the time and effort** traditionally spent on manual evaluation. By minimizing human error, NIRIKSHAK ensures **fairer and more consistent grading**. This makes the entire checking process not only **easier and faster** but also more reliable, allowing educators to focus more on teaching and less on administrative tasks.
